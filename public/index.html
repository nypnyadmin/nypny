Start Connecting!
NYPNY is a group for Nepalese young professionals residing in NY, NJ and CT area, who come together for three primary objectives.
Networking
Connecting people, forming relationships and generating opportunities through networking.
Mentorship
Fostering mentorship within the connected community.
Empowerment
Empowering for entrepreneurship and professional development so they can be the next generation business and community leaders.
